I have been living and travelling to Spain for over 30 years and have always loved and envied the culture and the scenery and stunning whitewashed villages and the wonderful vibrant Spanish Pottery that is available there.

Over the years I have bought back many individual pieces and built up a large collection. However friends often ask me where I bought the wonderful Spanish Pottery and often ask me to “bring some back for me”, hence this idea was born.

I am working with a company in Spain that have been producing hand painted Ceramics for 20 years.

They offer exclusive and traditional unique Spanish pottery in over 100 designs, in a typical Andalusian and Mediterranean style.

Each item is hand made and hand painted. You can build an enviable collection by buying items a few at a time, or you can purchase a whole dinner service in the assurance that the design you choose will always be available and will not disappear from the range after a limited period.
