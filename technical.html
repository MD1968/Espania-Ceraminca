Technical Information:

All products comply with international standards relating to the presence of lead and cadmium, and all of our ceramic products can be safely used in combination with food and drink.

There is no risk of poisoning.

Types of raw material/basic material used in the manual production process.

  • Terracotta clay
  • White clay
  • Ceramic paint
  • Glaze
  • Liquid white clay
